Description

MATSLISE is a graphical Matlab software package for the interactive numerical study of Sturm-Liouville problems. It allows the fast and accurate computation of the eigenvalues and the visualization of the corresponding eigenfunctions by making use of the power of high-order piecewise constant perturbation methods, also called the CP methods
